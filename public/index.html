About

InsurHacks is Toronto's premier hackathon for insurance innovation. Held at Symbility Intersect, the event features 12-hours of hacking, workshops, speakers, and pitching.

Our Goal

InsurHacks was created to encourage innovation in the field of insurance. We seek to bring people together to solve problems and challenges that plague the industry while offering resources and support to our participants. Join us on August 11, 2018 for Toronto's first open insurtech hackathon.

  • Pitch your project to industry experts in the insurtech space.
  • Compete for $2000 worth of prizes including mentorship, access to tech conferences, and startup resources.
  • Create innovation in areas such as P&C insurance, health, risk analysis, and data collection.
  • Network with other like-minded developers, designers, students, and innovators.

FAQs

How much does it cost?

InsurHacks is completely free for all attendees! We will also be providing meals, snacks, and drinks.

Who can attend?

InsurHacks is open to everyone, whether you are from industry or just a student. If you wish to become a mentor please email contact@omcn.tech.

How big can teams be?

Teams can have up to 4 people. Please note that prizes are given out on a per team basis. Team members will be responsible for dividing up the prizes.

How can my company get involved in this event?

We're always searching for sponsors and partners to improve the hackathon experience. Shoot us an email at contact@omcn.tech!

I have specific questions that are not addressed here. Who can help me?

Feel free to send us an email at contact@omcn.tech and we'll get back to you.
